Freight Shipment Tracker

Built with DeepSeek and Claude — an AI-assisted shipment tracking workflow for status, ETAs, exceptions, BOL/POD visibility, and operational monitoring.

The Problem

Shipment status was scattered across carrier websites, emails, and spreadsheets. There was no single source of truth, causing delays in exception detection.

The Solution

Built an AI-assisted shipment tracking workflow to organize shipment status, ETA updates, exception notes, BOL/POD visibility, operational remarks, and follow-up monitoring — using DeepSeek and Claude for workflow planning, feature structuring, logic refinement, and documentation.

The Result

100+ shipments tracked solo — delay detection cut from 48 hours to under 4 hours across 500+ daily transactions.
