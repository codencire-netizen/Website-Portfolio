Wholesale Sales Management

A full-stack office-ready system for wholesale sales — encoding, SOA generation, aging reports, role-based access, and audit monitoring in one secure platform.

The Problem

Wholesale operations relied on scattered spreadsheets for invoicing, receivables, and aging. No unified system, no audit trail, no role-based access control — leading to errors, duplicate entries, and compliance gaps.

The Solution

Built a comprehensive sales management system with multi-step encoding, customer profiles, SOA generation with aging buckets, role-based access (Admin, Encoder, Reviewer, Viewer), and full audit trail logging.

The Result

Single-platform system handling 3,300+ transactions — zero non-conformities across two ISO 9001:2015 external audits — deployed live on Vercel.

Key Features

Multi-Step Encoding

4-step invoice creation: document details, product lines, payment terms, and review. Auto-calculates totals, EWT, and receivables.

Statement of Account

Print-ready SOA with aging buckets (1-30, 30-60, 60-90, 90+ days), customer balance tracking, and payment status.

Aging Report

Receivables grouped by customer, document type, or representative. Filter by status, export to CSV/Excel, print-ready layout.

Role-Based Access

4 roles with granular permissions: Admin (full control), Encoder (create/edit), Reviewer (view/export), Viewer (read-only).

Audit Trail

Logs every action — edits, exports, cancellations, logins. Filterable by user, module, action type, with device tracking.

Customer Profiles

Reusable customer master data — name, address, TIN, payment terms, bank details. Autofills on transaction creation.
